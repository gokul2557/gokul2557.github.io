Our Story

A legacy of luxury and exceptional hospitality since 1995

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 1995, Luxe Hotel has been setting the standard for luxury hospitality for over two decades. What began as a boutique hotel has evolved into one of the most respected names in the industry.

Our commitment to providing exceptional service, coupled with our stunning location and world-class amenities, has earned us numerous accolades and the loyalty of guests from around the globe.

Today, we continue to innovate and enhance our offerings while maintaining the timeless elegance and personal touch that has become our hallmark.
